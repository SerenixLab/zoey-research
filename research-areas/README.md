# Capability-Family Research Areas

Research-area records begin from a capability-map gap and coordinate legacy audit, external search, comparative requirements, and candidate sequencing.

They are working research frames, not architecture, roadmap, adoption, or workbench authorization. Repository names may remain in a research-area shortlist while unreviewed; once a project enters source-bounded candidate review it must be registered in [`../CANDIDATE_REGISTER.md`](../CANDIDATE_REGISTER.md) and receive its own record under [`../external-capabilities/`](../external-capabilities/README.md).
